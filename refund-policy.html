Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

We want you to feel good about subscribing to Resurface. If it's not for you, we make refunds simple.

14-day, no-questions-asked window

If you're within 14 days of your most recent payment, you're entitled to a full refund, no questions asked. Just email us and we'll process it.

How to request a refund

Email n0nyf0xy@duck.com with the email address used at checkout. Refunds are typically processed within a few business days through Paddle, our payment processor.

After 14 days

Requests made after the 14-day window are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. We're generally reasonable about genuine issues (billing errors, technical problems preventing use of the Service), but we don't guarantee refunds for later requests.

Cancelling vs. refunding

Cancelling your subscription stops future billing but does not by itself refund past charges. If you'd like both, mention that in your email.
