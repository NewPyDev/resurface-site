Resurface

Save it for later. Actually come back to it.

A Chrome extension that saves pages you want to read, then gently resurfaces them on your new tab page until you do.

  • Save any page with one click or right-click
  • One saved item resurfaces on every new tab — no digging through bookmarks
  • Snooze, pin, or mark done, right from the card
  • Everything stored locally in your browser — no ads, no tracking
